üéØ What is MDM2?

MDM2 (Mouse Double Minute 2) is a crucial protein that regulates p53, known as the "guardian of the genome." In cancer cells, MDM2 often blocks p53's tumor suppressor functions. MDM2 inhibitors can restore p53 activity, making them promising anti-cancer therapeutics. Our AI model predicts which molecules are likely to inhibit MDM2, accelerating drug discovery.
